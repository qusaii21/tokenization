# SentencePiece Tokenization

SentencePiece treats text as raw bytes and trains a subword vocab without needing whitespace. :contentReference[oaicite:14]{index=14}

## Features

- Supports multiple algorithms (BPE + Unigram)
- Learns vocab directly from raw text
- Good for languages without spaces

SentencePiece is widely used in modern NLP pipelines due to flexibility and robustness in multilingual setups. :contentReference[oaicite:15]{index=15}
